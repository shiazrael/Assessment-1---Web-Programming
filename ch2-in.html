Information

Following success, Moonton has tapped into the esports scene with the creation of several regional tournaments dubbed as Mobile Legends: Bang Bang Professional League (MPL) that serves as a qualifier for the Mobile Legends World Championships. It was among the six games chosen for the first medal event of esports competition at the 2019 Southeast Asian Games (SEA) held in the Philippines. The game was also a medal event in the last SEA Games in Cambodia and will be one too in 2025, to be held in Thailand.
